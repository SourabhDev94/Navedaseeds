Ash gourd Bharat F1, NA-677

1. Uniform cylindrical green fruits turning waxi white at maturity.
2. Fruit: 6-8 Kg, thick, Milky white, Smooth flesh, High flash recovery.
3. Harvesting: 70-75 days of sowing.
4. Suitable for long shipping.

Ash gourd Boxer F1, NA-672

1. Uniform cylindrical light green fruits turning waxi white at maturity.
2. Fruit: 10-12 Kg, thick, Milky white, High flash recovery.
3. Harvesting: 75-80 days of sowing.
4. Suitable for long shipping.
